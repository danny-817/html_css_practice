V

Mercenary, Edgerunner, professional quilter, menace to Night City?

If you like to keep your head down, and you're content with life in Night City, you might not have heard of V, but if you've ever tried to make it big on the streets, you'll know to fear the name.

Some of V's Hobbies

  • Considerate driving
  • Mixing up the laundry baskets
  • Calling people "Jabroni"

V's known crimes

  1. Knocking over a recycling bin
  2. Upsetting pigeons
  3. Raising his voice near a care home
  4. Stealing a bag of crisps

As you can see from these, V is not someone you want to trifle with. These are just his known crimes, undoubtedly there are countless other heinous acts that have gone unreported.
